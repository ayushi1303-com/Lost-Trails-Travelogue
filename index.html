ABOUT US

We are a team of college students driven by a passion for travel and technology, aiming to create a unique platform for exploring India's lesser-known destinations. Our website is designed to guide adventurers to offbeat locations, offering rich insights into local food, culture, and history while making trip planning easy and engaging. With an emphasis on affordability, accessibility, and personalization, we want to bring you closer to the hidden gems of India and redefine the way you experience travel.

KONNI

Konni, a serene town in Kerala, is renowned for its lush forests and elephant training center, offering a glimpse into Kerala's wildlife heritage. It is a gateway to the Western Ghats, known for trekking, spice plantations, and eco-tourism.

KODAIKANAL

Kodaikanal, often called the "Princess of Hill Stations," is a charming retreat in Tamil Nadu, nestled in the misty hills of the Western Ghats. The cool climate, vibrant flora, and attractions like Bryant Park and Pillar Rocks make it a popular destination.

TRICHY

Tiruchirappalli, commonly known as Trichy, is a historic city in Tamil Nadu known for its ancient temples and cultural heritage. Dominated by the impressive Rockfort Temple, it also houses the iconic Ranganathaswamy Temple in nearby Srirangam, one of the largest temple complexes in India.
